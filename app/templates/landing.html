Welcome to E-vote

A Blockchain-Based Secure Voting Platform

Getting Started

Follow these steps to cast your vote securely:

  1. Register: Ensure you are registered with a valid Voter ID.
  2. Select Candidate and fill details: Choose the candidate you wish to vote and fill in your credentials.
  3. Cast Vote: Review your selection and submit your vote.
  4. Confirmation: Receive confirmation that your vote has been securely recorded on the blockchain.
Features
  • Secure and tamper-proof voting process using blockchain technology
  • Easy-to-use interface for all voters
  • Immediate confirmation of vote submission
